Flood Analysis

A GIS-based analysis of potential flooding using elevation data, water-level scenarios, and urban building information.

Project Overview

This assignment explores how flooding could affect an urban area by combining elevation data with building and land surface information. The analysis uses GIS tools to visualise areas that may be more vulnerable under different flood-height scenarios.

Flood Visualisation

3D flood analysis visualisation showing buildings, terrain, and simulated flood extent.

This visualisation shows the final result of my flood analysis. I used elevation data to create several flood-height zones and displayed them together with building data in a 3D scene. The coloured areas show the simulated spread of water across lower-lying parts of the study area, while the building blocks help us see which buildings would be damaged
